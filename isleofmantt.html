A little about the Isle of man TT

Every year, a small island in between England and Ireland called the Isle of Man is converted into one of the fastest circuits in the world. The Isle of Man TT are a series of motorcycle races that race on a street circuit that roughly covers the perimeter of the island and is one of the fastest tracks in the world.
